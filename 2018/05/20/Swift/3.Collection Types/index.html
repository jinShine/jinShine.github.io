Subset & Superset

  • isSubset(of:): subset인지를 판별 (부분 집합)
  • isSuperset(of:): superset인지를 판별 (상위 집합)
  • isStrictSubset(of:): subset이면서, subset!=superset
  • isStrictSuperset(of:): superset이면서, subset!=superset
  • isDisjoint(with:): 두 Set 간의 교집합이 없음
